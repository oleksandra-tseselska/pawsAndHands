About us


Our “Paws & Hands” website is a big pet owners', pet lovers' and pet service providers' community and social network with a great functionality.

Have you ever thought where to find handler, groomer, veterinarian or other service provider for your pet? Or maybe you are going to find a friend/couple for your pet or take a new pet at home? Wouldn't you also be happy to get discounts for great quality pet services, accessories and food as well as get new friends that are passionate about cats or dogs so much as you are?

If your answer is "yes", we are happy to announce that you are in the right place! In our website you can create profiles for you and your pets, see other users and their pets, communicate with them, see pets looking for dates or new homes and even reserve pets from pet shelters. Also you have an ability to see events for pets and their owners as well as to create new ones by yourself. And don't forget to visit "Discounts" section where you can find great offers that are available only for members of our community.

If you haven't registered yet let's do it right now. Our pet lover community is waiting for you. Let's connect paws and hands together!
